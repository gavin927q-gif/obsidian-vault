# Cold Call & Walk-In Scripts — Web Design Pitches

> These scripts are built around each business's specific problem. Always have the proposal PDF ready on your phone or printed. Lead with their problem, not your service.
>
> **Your proof:** hillsborotireservice.com — pull it up on your phone, show it in 10 seconds.
> **Your ask:** Not "buy a website." Just: "Can I show you what I have in mind?"

---

## HOW TO USE THESE

- **Walk-in:** Print the proposal or have it on your phone. Go in, ask for the owner by name, use the script.
- **Cold call:** Use the phone script word for word. Keep it under 90 seconds.
- **Email follow-up:** If they say "send me something," send the PDF the same day with a short note.
- **The golden rule:** Shut up after you ask the closing question. Let them answer.

"I'm not asking you to make any decisions today. I just want to know — does this look like something worth a 20-minute conversation?"

### Objection Handlers

- **"We already have a website."** → "I know — I looked at it before coming in. That's actually why I'm here. It's on Wix, which is holding your SEO back. I'd love to show you the difference."
