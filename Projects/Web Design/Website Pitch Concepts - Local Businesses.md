# Website Pitch Concepts — Local Business Targets

> These are researched, site-specific pitch ideas for each contact. Every concept is based on what their current site actually looks like and what's missing. Use these to personalize your outreach or walk-in pitch.

---

## 1. 🏚️ Swift Realty — Ryan
**Current site:** swiftrealty.net
**Built with:** Custom (decent bones — dark header, warm tones, Work Sans / PT Serif)
**Suggested price:** $3,500–$5,500

### What They Have
A reasonably professional site. Clean layout, good branding, rehab project pages, RV park listings. Better than average for a small Hillsboro investor.

### What's Missing / Weak
- No before/after photo galleries on rehab projects — the whole value of flipping is visual, and it's buried in text
- RV park booking is not obvious — Cedar Ridge (Glen Rose) and Open Valley (Cresson) should have their own landing pages with availability, rates, and a direct booking or contact form
- No team or owner bio — 20+ years of experience isn't mentioned prominently
- No testimonials or reviews from tenants/buyers
- No blog or market update content — no SEO value being built

### The Pitch
> "Ryan, your site looks solid but it's not working as hard as it could be. Your rehab projects don't show before/afters — which is your biggest selling point. And your RV parks don't have their own pages with booking info. You're leaving money on the table. I can build you a full rebuild with dedicated RV park landing pages, a before/after gallery, and a proper investor profile. People who visit your site should want to do business with you before they ever pick up the phone."

### What to Build
- **Homepage** — Strong hero, quick intro to Ryan/Swift Realty, 3 service blocks (We Buy Houses / RV Parks / Rehab Projects)
- **Rehab Projects Gallery** — Before/after sliders for each property with sale info
- **RV Parks** — Separate page per park (Cedar Ridge, Open Valley) with photos, rates, amenities map, contact/booking form
- **About Ryan** — 20+ years, local Hillsboro investor, personal story
- **Testimonials** — Tenant and seller reviews
- **Contact** — Simple form + phone prominently

## 2. 🏠 Herring Properties
**Current site:** herringproperties.com
**Built with:** Wix (template — generic, no differentiation)
**Suggested price:** $2,500–$4,000

### What They Have
A standard Wix template site. Functional but completely generic — you could swap their name out and it could be any realtor. No personality, no local identity, no differentiation.

### What's Missing / Weak
- Built on Wix — slow, hard to update, terrible SEO
- No agent profiles or team pages
- No listings showcase
- No "why Herring" differentiation
- No testimonials
- Doesn't reflect their 20+ years in Hillsboro or Chamber membership
- info@herringproperties.com is the only contact — no faces, no names

### The Pitch
> "I looked at your site — it's built on Wix, which is holding you back. It loads slow, Google doesn't rank it well, and honestly it doesn't stand out. For a firm that's been in Hillsboro as long as you have, you deserve a site that shows that. I can build you something custom — with your team's faces, your listings, your local story — that actually brings people in."

### What to Build
- **Homepage** — Local Hillsboro hero image, "Trusted in Hill County Since [year]", service overview
- **Meet the Team** — Agent bios with photos, credentials, specialties
- **Listings** — IDX or manual listing showcase with photos
- **Buyer / Seller Pages** — Simple guides showing how Herring helps each
- **Testimonials** — Local client reviews
- **Contact** — Map, phone, individual agent emails, contact form
- **Blog** — Hillsboro market updates (optional, big SEO boost)

## 3. 🏭 ORB Investments TX — Amanda Marbut
**Current site:** orbinvesttx.com
**Built with:** WordPress (WP Rocket, Gravity Forms)
**Suggested price:** $4,000–$7,000

### What They Have
A WordPress site focused on a single commercial unit (Unit 116). Professional photography, clean blue/white design, carousel gallery, contact form. The bones are decent but the scope is tiny.

### What's Missing / Weak
- Only showcases ONE unit — if they have multiple properties, none are visible
- No company history, no "About ORB" page, no team info
- No virtual tour — standard for commercial real estate now
- No SEO content — one-page basically
- No tenant testimonials or case studies
- Missing the IH-35 corridor angle as a selling point (DFW–Waco traffic is a major asset)
- No leasing inquiry workflow — just a basic contact form

### The Pitch
> "Amanda, your site only shows one unit right now. If ORB has more properties — or will have more — you need a site that can grow with you. I can build a full commercial real estate showcase with a property portfolio, your IH-35 location as a major headline, virtual tours, and a proper leasing inquiry system. Something that makes developers and tenants take you seriously before they even call."

### What to Build
- **Homepage** — Bold hero: "Prime Commercial Space. Hillsboro, TX. IH-35 Corridor." Stats: sq footage, location advantage, proximity to DFW/Waco
- **Properties** — Individual pages per unit/property with photo galleries, floor plans, specs, virtual tour embed
- **Location Advantage Page** — IH-35, HWY 35E/35W merge, traffic counts, proximity to major markets
- **About ORB** — Company story, team, investment philosophy
- **Tenant Testimonials** — Social proof for commercial tenants
- **Leasing Inquiry Form** — Structured form: company name, space needed, timeline, use type
- **Contact** — Amanda direct + phone prominently

But nobody can tell that from your website. I can build you a personal brand site that's 100% yours — your face, your story, your markets — with neighborhood guides for every city you work in. - **Testimonials** — Client reviews front and center

## 5. 🌾 Jana Nors Real Estate
**Current site:** jananorsrealestate.com
**Built with:** WordPress / Bootstrap (dark brown + gold + burgundy)
**Suggested price:** $3,000–$5,000

### What They Have
A custom WordPress site with a distinct dark/gold aesthetic — more personality than most local realtors. Has property listings, search/filter, testimonials section, map integration. Better than average.

### What's Missing / Weak
- Navigation is complex and confusing on mobile — submenus don't work well
- Contact form is hard to find
- 30+ year career and deep Hill County roots aren't front and center
- Farm, ranch, and land listings deserve a completely different experience than residential — they're not treated differently
- No dedicated farm/ranch landing page
- Heavy page load due to Bootstrap framework
- No blog or market update content despite being a local expert since 1987

### The Pitch
> "Jana, your site has more personality than most around here — I like the dark and gold look. But the mobile navigation is a mess, and for someone with 30+ years in Hill County, your story isn't front and center. More importantly — your farm and ranch listings deserve their own experience. A buyer looking for 100 acres of Hill County land isn't the same as someone buying a house in Hillsboro. I can rebuild this with a dedicated farm/ranch section, a cleaner mobile experience, and your legacy front and center."

### What to Build
- **Homepage** — Jana's 30+ year story as the hero. "Hill County's Farm & Ranch Expert Since 1987." Strong photography of land/rural TX
- **Farm & Ranch** — Dedicated section with acreage listings, aerial photos, soil/water info, rural property buying guide
- **Residential** — Separate clean section for homes in Hillsboro, Abbott, surrounding areas
- **About Jana** — Full bio, Baylor connection, born and raised Hill County story, why she does this
- **Area Map** — Visual coverage map: Hill County, McLennan County, Central Texas
- **Testimonials** — Pull from existing + add new
- **Blog** — "Hill County Land Market" updates — major SEO play for farm/ranch searches
- **Contact** — Simple, prominent, direct to Jana

## 6. 🏢 WestLand Realty Group — Katie Miller + Katie Smith
**Current site:** westlandrg.com
**Built with:** Custom (navy + red, Poppins/Inter — most polished of the group)
**Suggested price:** $6,000–$10,000

### What They Have
The most developed site in this group. Navy/red branding, team profiles, neighborhood guides (9+ areas), blog, home valuation tool, testimonial carousel, property search. Genuinely solid.

### What's Missing / Weak
- Property search is basic — no advanced filters, no map search
- No virtual tours on listings
- Mobile interactive elements have issues
- No client login portal for active buyers/sellers to track their transaction
- No FAQ section
- Mortgage calculator exists but unclear if functional
- Blog content exists but could be much more targeted to Hill County buyers
- No video content — no agent intro videos, no property walkthrough videos

### The Pitch
> "Katie, your site is genuinely the best in this market — but 'best in Hillsboro' isn't the bar you should be shooting for. You have neighborhood guides, team profiles, a valuation tool — great. But your property search is basic, you have no virtual tours, no client portal, and your mobile experience has some rough spots. I do premium rebuilds for firms like yours — we're talking $6,000–$10,000 range — that take what you have and turn it into something that competes with regional firms in Waco and beyond."

### What to Build
- **Homepage** — Elevated design, stronger hero, instant home value CTA, featured agents
- **Advanced Property Search** — Map-based search, advanced filters (acreage, school district, price range, waterfront, etc.)
- **Virtual Tours** — Matterport or video embed per listing
- **Client Portal** — Login for active clients to see documents, timeline, and transaction updates
- **Neighborhood Guides** — Expand from 9 to 15+ with school data, market stats, lifestyle info per area
- **Agent Profiles** — Video introduction per agent, full bio, production stats
- **FAQ** — Buyer/Seller common questions
- **Blog** — SEO-targeted: "Homes for sale in West TX", "Hill County real estate market" etc.
- **Mobile Overhaul** — Full responsive audit and rebuild of interactive elements
