# Compliance & Legal

> [!warning] Licensed Sub Required
> Never advertise "plumbing" or "electrical" work. Always say **"minor fixture swaps."** Always note that a licensed subcontractor is used. This is a Texas legal requirement.

> [!warning] Thomas Smith Must Sign
> Until Gavin turns 18, **Thomas Smith (father) must sign all contracts, LLC paperwork**, and be joint holder on the business bank account. Gavin is co-operator only.

> [!warning] Business Bank Account
> Thomas must be joint account holder. Do not open account without Thomas present.

## Texas LLC Notes
- Filing fee: ~$300 with Texas Secretary of State
- Registered agent required (can be Thomas or a service)
- EIN needed after LLC is formed (free via IRS.gov)
- General liability insurance required before taking commercial jobs
