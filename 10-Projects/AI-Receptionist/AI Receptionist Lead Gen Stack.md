# AI Receptionist / Lead Gen Stack

## Concept
Automated lead scraping + AI phone receptionist sold to local businesses as a service. Pairs well as an upsell on web design clients.

## Stack
| Tool | Role |
|------|------|
| VAPI or Synthflow | AI phone receptionist (inbound/outbound calls) |
| Outscraper | Lead scraping — local business contact data |
| Google Docs automation | CRM / lead tracking output |

## Target Customers
Local businesses that miss calls or need 24/7 coverage:
- Auto shops
- Home service businesses
- Restaurants
- Clinics / offices

## Business Model
- Monthly SaaS fee per client
- Bundle with web design package for higher LTV
