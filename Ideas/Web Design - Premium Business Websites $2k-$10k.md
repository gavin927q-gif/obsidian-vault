# Idea: Premium Website Rebuilds for Established Businesses — $2,000–$10,000

## The Idea
Shift a portion of web design focus toward larger, established local and regional businesses that have outdated or low-quality websites. Instead of $500–$700 starter sites, offer full professional redesigns at premium price points — $2,000 to $10,000+ per project.

## Why It Makes Sense
- One $5,000 job = 7–10 small jobs worth of revenue
- Established businesses already have revenue — they can pay
- Many local businesses in Hillsboro, Waco, and Hill County have embarrassingly outdated websites
- Professional redesign = stronger ROI argument ("your site is costing you customers")
- Proof of concept: Hillsboro Tire is now live — use it as a portfolio anchor

## Target Client Profile
- Businesses with 5–50 employees
- Existing website that looks 10+ years old
- Active on Facebook but website doesn't match their real quality
- Local and regional: Hillsboro, Waco, Corsicana, Cleburne, Hillsboro corridor
- Industries: law firms, medical practices, HVAC/plumbing, auto shops, real estate firms, restaurants, contractors

## Pricing Tiers (Draft)
| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Professional Refresh | $2,000–$3,500 | New design, mobile-optimized, modern stack, SEO basics |
| Full Rebuild | $4,000–$6,500 | Custom design, CMS, contact forms, Google integration, photography direction |
| Premium Brand + Web | $7,000–$10,000+ | Full brand refresh, custom site, SEO setup, Google Business, social assets |

## The Pitch Angle
> "Your website is the first thing a customer sees. If it looks like 2010, they assume your business does too. I build modern, professional websites that actually convert — and I'm local."
